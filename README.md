## How It Works

**Pick your city**  
Search any city worldwide or use your current location.

**Set preferences**  
Choose duration, style, pace, and language for your ideal trip.

**AI generates your tour**  
In seconds, get a complete itinerary with stops, tips, and routes.

**Explore & navigate**  
Follow your tour with one-tap navigation. Save it or export to PDF.

## Features

**Personalized Walking Tours**  
Get custom itineraries tailored to your preferences in seconds.

**GPS Navigation**  
One-tap navigation to guide you through your tour.

**Save & Sync**  
Create a free account to save tours and access them across all your devices.

**Export to PDF**  
Take your tours offline with PDF export.

**Worldwide Coverage**  
Generate tours for any city around the globe.

**Multilingual Support**  
Create tours in your preferred language.
